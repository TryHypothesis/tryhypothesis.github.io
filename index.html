request for startups

Are you a Triangle area startup team planning to launch new technology in 2016? Apply today to take advantage of valuable customer development marketing research. Three triangle teams will be able to learn valuable insight into their target market and prospective customers. Apply Now!

deadline

March 5, 2016

criteria

A committee of technology and startup experts will screen each application ensuring all requirements are met and decide which product is most innovative and in alignment with the sponsor’s framework.
Applicants must be located within the Triangle area (Durham, Orange & Wake County) of North Carolina.
Only early stage, pre-launch, or not yet operational technology product companies will be considered.
Applicant must describe a new business or an early stage company

description

hypothesis has developed a unique platform that market tests ‘ideas' for technology products that enables entrepreneurs to understand their market so they are building products that others actually want to buy. We are offering three Triangle are tech startups the opportunity to join our alpha.

eligibility

To enter you must meet the following requirements
Be a technology startup (including not yet operational), less than 2 years old with an idea or actual technology product.
Be located within the Triangle area of North Carolina.
Be able to meet in person on occasion in our Durham offices.
Agree to the following terms:
Applications may be submitted by individuals or by teams, but all applicants must be 21 years of age or older.
Applicants must be located within the Triangle area of North Carolina.
Only early stage, pre-launch, or not yet operational technology product or service companies will be considered.
All Applicants must be the original work of those making the submission.
Applications must describe a new business or an early stage company.
All Applicants must available for interview with selection committee.
Partially completed entry applications or those submitted after deadline will not be accepted.
The decisions of the hypothesis are final, including interpretation of the rules.
Applicants must be available to commence market research activities by end of March/early April 2016.
All selections including interpretation of the elibibility are final and are within complete purview of selection committee.
Applicants also acknowledge hypothesis is an alpha product and agree to provide both input into the marketing process and well as feedback and being available to meet weekly during the service period.
The value of the marketing research services provided to the winners during the 30 day period is valued between $25,000 and $35,000. Selected winners will execute a convertible note with Hypothesis for $25,000 due within 12 months for marketing services rendered prior to service period. Winners may also convert to hypothesis clients at close of marketing period.

decision

hypothesis alpha market research platform for 4 weeks, starting end of March 2016 with at market value of $35k. Three awards to be announced on Monday, March 14, 2016.
